Research

I look at numbers and tell you a story

I am currently a PhD student in Economics at the Department of Business and Economics at the University of Naples Parthenope. My research focuses on inequality and long-run growth, with strong interests in culture, economic history, and econometrics.

I look at numbers and tell stories about them, exploring the patterns and relationships that shape economic outcomes across time and societies. My work seeks to understand the fundamental mechanisms that drive economic development and how historical forces continue to influence contemporary economic structures.
